## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips for Each Scenario

### **Customer Service Prompts**
- **Empathy Integration**: Include emotional intelligence and active listening patterns
- **Brand Consistency**: Ensure brand voice and value alignment
- **Escalation Clarity**: Define clear escalation paths and procedures
- **Personalization**: Adapt to customer history and context

### **Medical Diagnosis Prompts**
- **Safety First**: Build in conservative diagnosis and referral protocols
- **Compliance Rigorous**: Strict HIPAA and medical regulation adherence
- **Accuracy Validation**: Multi-layer verification and confidence scoring
- **Continuous Learning**: Update with latest medical research and guidelines

### **Code Review Prompts**
- **Security Comprehensive**: Cover OWASP Top 10 and common vulnerabilities
- **Performance Focused**: Identify bottlenecks and optimization opportunities
- **Maintainability**: Assess code clarity, documentation, and structure
- **Integration Ready**: Seamlessly integrate with existing development workflows

### **Educational Content Prompts**
- **Learning Science**: Apply evidence-based pedagogical principles
- **Adaptive Design**: Support multiple learning styles and paces
- **Engagement Creative**: Use storytelling and real-world examples
- **Assessment Integrated**: Include learning verification and feedback mechanisms

### **Financial Analysis Prompts**
- **Regulatory Strict**: Ensure SOX, GAAP, and industry compliance
- **Accuracy Critical**: Multi-level verification and error detection
- **Communication Clear**: Stakeholder-appropriate language and visualization
- **Audit Ready**: Complete documentation and traceability

### **Creative Writing Prompts**
- **Author Voice**: Preserve and enhance unique writing style
- **Story Structure**: Apply narrative theory and storytelling principles
- **Character Depth**: Support complex character development and arcs
- **Creative Freedom**: Provide inspiration without imposing constraints
